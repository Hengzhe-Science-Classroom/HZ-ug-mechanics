# Mechanics · Interactive

**Rigorous Newtonian mechanics for physics majors**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 3 · Undergraduate

## What You'll Learn

- Vectors, kinematics, Newton's laws (rigorous)
- Work-energy theorem, conservative forces
- Momentum, center of mass, collisions
- Rotation, moment of inertia, angular momentum
- Oscillations and normal modes
- Central force motion and orbits

## References

- Daniel Kleppner & Robert Kolenkow, *An Introduction to Mechanics* (2nd ed., Cambridge)
- John R. Taylor, *Classical Mechanics* (introductory level)
- Morin, *Introduction to Classical Mechanics* (Cambridge)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
